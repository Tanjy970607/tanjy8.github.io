# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Potential freelance clients, platform reviewers, and collaborators who need a fast read on Je Yen Tan's services, credibility, pricing, and contact path.

## Product Purpose
This portfolio presents Je Yen Tan as an independent freelancer for data entry, trilingual translation, PowerPoint design, and Python data work. Success means visitors understand the offer quickly, trust the work quality, and can request a quote without confusion.

## Brand Personality
Precise, capable, polished. The site should feel like a premium work machine: clear, responsive, technical, and practical.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic AI landing-page patterns, emoji-heavy service cards, fake sent states, unreadable gray text, busy gradient text, excessive glow, and decorative effects that make the portfolio look like a template.

## Design Principles
- Show capability through concrete service examples and working mini-tools.
- Make the first viewport feel premium and focused, with the name and core services immediately clear.
- Keep interactions honest: forms and widgets should only claim actions that really happen.
- Use restrained motion and hardware-inspired visuals to support a premium laptop-workbench direction without copying any specific brand.
- Preserve readability across desktop and mobile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly controls, visible focus states, responsive text that does not overflow, and reduced-motion fallbacks for animation-heavy areas.
